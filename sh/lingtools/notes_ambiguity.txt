The new code requires:

Local installation of WordNet 2.1:
http://wordnetcode.princeton.edu/2.1/WordNet-2.1.tar.gz
- only the "/dict" folder is required. 

2 additional libraries: -both are in "ling/coordination/WordNet Dependencies"
jwnl.jar
JWordNetSim.jar

2 related files: -both are in "ling/coordination/WordNet Dependencies"
ic-bnc-resnik.dat
wordnet.xml
- these are used by the method semanticSim() in coordAmbiguity.class.
- the parameter "dictionary_path" in wordnet.xml must point to the WordNet 2.1 "/dict" folder.
